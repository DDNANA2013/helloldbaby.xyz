How to Play

Master the art of survival in Monster Survivors:

  • • Use WASD or arrow keys to move
  • • Click to attack monsters
  • • Collect power-ups and upgrades
  • • Survive wave after wave of enemies
  • • Unlock new abilities and weapons

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an engaging online survival game that puts your skills to the test. As a brave survivor, you'll face endless waves of monsters while collecting resources, upgrading your abilities, and trying to stay alive as long as possible. The game features smooth controls, beautiful graphics, and an addictive progression system that keeps you coming back for more.
